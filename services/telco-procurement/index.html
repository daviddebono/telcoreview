Telco Procurement

Telco procurement can be time-consuming and confusing. We help Australian businesses compare quotes, evaluate providers and make informed procurement decisions—for business phone, NBN, mobile, contact centre and 1300/1800 services—without favouring any single vendor.

What We Offer

Our procurement support can include:

  • Defining requirements and drafting request-for-quote or tender documentation,
  • Shortlisting providers based on capability, coverage and typical pricing,
  • Comparing quotes on a like-for-like basis (features, SLAs, contract terms, cost),
  • Clarifying technical and commercial terms so you can compare fairly,
  • Support during negotiation and contract finalisation.

We do not sell carrier services. We advise so you can run a transparent process and select the option that best fits your organisation.

When Procurement Support Helps

Procurement support is useful when you are replacing an existing provider, adding new services, or consolidating multiple contracts. It is also valuable when internal teams lack the time or market knowledge to compare providers effectively. We work with businesses across Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra, Hobart, Perth and regional Australia.

Outcomes

You get a clear comparison of options and a recommended direction (or shortlist), with reasoning based on your requirements and budget. You then engage the chosen provider(s) directly. We can assist with transition planning and, where appropriate, ongoing review at contract renewal.
