Contact Centre

Contact centre and call-handling solutions combine telephony, queuing, reporting and often CRM integration. If your current platform is underperforming or you are reviewing alternatives, Telco Review can help you compare options from multiple Australian and international providers.

Why review your contact centre?

Contact centre technology has evolved: cloud platforms now offer flexibility, scalability and often simpler deployment than legacy on-premise systems. If you are experiencing reliability issues, outgrowing your current solution, or facing a contract renewal, an independent review can map the market without favouring one vendor. We focus on capabilities and fit—not on criticising your incumbent.

We work with organisations across Sydney (North Sydney, Parramatta, Chatswood, Macquarie Park, Sydney CBD), Melbourne (CBD, Richmond, South Yarra, Docklands), Brisbane (CBD, Fortitude Valley, South Brisbane), Adelaide, Canberra and Hobart. Whether you have a single site or distributed agents, we can help you compare typical offerings and deployment models.

What’s typically available

Cloud contact centre — Full-featured platforms delivered from the cloud: ACD, IVR, recording, reporting, workforce management. Many providers offer Australian data centres and local support. We compare typical feature sets and integration options.

Telephony and SIP — Contact centre platforms often work with multiple telephony providers. We can help you understand how trunking and inbound numbers (including 1300/1800) fit with different solutions.

Hybrid and legacy — Some organisations need to integrate with existing PABX or gradual migration. We can discuss what’s typically possible and how providers compare.

What we compare

We do not sell contact centre platforms. We compare typical capabilities, deployment options, support and contract terms across a range of providers. If your current solution is not meeting your needs, we help you see what alternatives can offer—without negative commentary about your incumbent. Our aim is clear, factual comparison so you can shortlist and then engage vendors directly.

Next steps

Request a consultation and we’ll discuss your agent count, locations (including suburbs in Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra and Hobart), current platform and goals. We can then outline how a review would work and what options we’d compare.
