1300 / 1800 Inbound Numbers

1300 and 1800 numbers are a standard way for Australian businesses to present a single, memorable inbound number with flexible routing. If your current provider is not meeting your needs or you are reviewing options, Telco Review can help you compare inbound number providers.

Why review your 1300/1800 arrangement?

Inbound number services vary in pricing, routing options, reporting and integration with your phone system or contact centre. If you are paying more than you’d like, need better reporting, or want to consolidate with other telco services, an independent review can map the market without favouring one provider. We focus on capabilities and fit—not on criticising your incumbent.

We work with organisations across Sydney (North Sydney, Parramatta, Chatswood, Sydney CBD), Melbourne (CBD, Richmond, South Yarra, Docklands), Brisbane (CBD, Fortitude Valley, South Brisbane), Adelaide, Canberra and Hobart. Inbound numbers are national; we can compare providers that serve businesses in all these locations.

What’s typically available

Number provisioning — Many Australian carriers and specialist providers offer 1300 and 1800 number provisioning. Availability of specific number ranges varies; we can outline typical options.

Routing and features — Time-based routing, geographic routing, overflow and queue integration are common. We compare typical feature sets so you understand what’s available.

Reporting and billing — Call detail, duration and cost reporting differ by provider. Some integrate with contact centre platforms; we can discuss typical capabilities.

What we compare

We do not sell inbound number services. We compare typical pricing, features, reporting and integration options across a range of Australian providers. If your current arrangement is not meeting your needs, we help you see what alternatives can offer—without negative commentary about your incumbent. Our aim is clear, factual comparison so you can choose with confidence.

Single-bill aggregation

Where it suits, we can help you consolidate 1300/1800 with other services (e.g. phone, internet, mobile) on one bill. This can simplify administration; we can discuss whether it’s relevant to you.

Next steps

Request a consultation and we’ll discuss your current inbound setup, locations (including suburbs in Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra and Hobart) and goals. We can then outline how a review would work and what options we’d compare.
