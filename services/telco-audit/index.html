Telco Audit

A Telco Audit gives you a clear picture of what you are spending, what you are getting, and where opportunities exist. We analyse your bills, contracts and usage so you can make informed decisions—whether you stay with your current provider or explore alternatives.

What a Telco Audit Covers

Our audit process typically includes:

  • Review of current providers and services (phone, internet, mobile, 1300/1800, contact centre),
  • Analysis of bills and charges to identify errors, overpayment or better plans,
  • Contract review: terms, renewal dates, exit costs and flexibility,
  • Assessment of service levels and support against your requirements,
  • Identification of consolidation or technology options that could reduce cost or improve fit.

We do not sell carrier services. Our audit is impartial so you receive an unbiased view of your telecommunications spend and options.

Who It's For

Australian businesses and organisations that want clarity on their telco spend—whether you have a single site or multiple offices across Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra, Hobart, Perth or regional areas. An audit is especially useful when contracts are nearing renewal, when you have grown or restructured, or when you suspect you are paying more than you need to.

Outcomes

You receive a structured assessment and, where relevant, recommendations. These might include renegotiating with your incumbent, switching provider, consolidating services onto one bill, or changing technology (e.g. moving to a hosted phone system or reviewing NBN tiers). Our goal is to put forward options that are value for money and fit your operations.
