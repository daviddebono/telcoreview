About Us

Telco Review provides independent, vendor-agnostic telecommunications review and advice to Australian businesses and corporate customers.

Our approach

We do not sell carrier services ourselves. We compare business-grade phone systems, internet (including NBN), mobiles, contact centre solutions and inbound numbers (1300/1800) across a range of Australian and international providers with an Australian presence. If your current provider is not meeting your needs or you are simply reviewing your options, we help you explore alternatives without negative commentary about incumbents—we focus on capabilities and fit.

Who we work with

We work with businesses across Australia—including Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra and Hobart, and their major suburbs. Our engagements range from one-off reviews to ongoing advisory and procurement support.

Why use us

  • Vendor-agnostic: we compare options, we don't push one brand.
  • Single-bill aggregation where it suits your organisation.
  • Clear, factual comparisons to support your decision-making.
