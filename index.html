What We Do

Telco Review provides vendor-agnostic telecommunications advice to Australian businesses. We do not favour one carrier; we compare mainstream and alternative providers so you can make informed decisions. If your incumbent services are not meeting your needs, we help you identify alternatives and, where appropriate, consolidate services on one bill.

We specialise in business-grade phone systems, internet (NBN and alternatives), mobiles, contact centre solutions, and inbound numbers (1300/1800). Our process is transparent and focused on your requirements—not on pushing a single brand.

Who It's For

Australian businesses and corporate customers who are:

  • Considering a review of their current telecommunications arrangements
  • Looking for an alternative provider due to service issues or contract end
  • Seeking one consolidated bill across multiple services
  • Evaluating business phone systems, NBN, mobiles, or contact centre options

We service organisations across Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra and Hobart—including major suburbs and regional centres.

How It Works

A simple, low-pressure process:

  1. Enquiry — You contact us with your current setup and goals (e.g. review at contract end, consolidation, or exploring alternatives).
  2. Discovery — We discuss your services (phone, internet, mobile, contact centre, 1300/1800), locations and priorities.
  3. Options — We present alternatives from a range of providers, with clear pros and capabilities—no vendor bashing.
  4. Decision — You choose. We can support transition and, where applicable, single-bill aggregation.

Engagement Models

We offer flexible engagement to suit your size and needs:

  • One-off review — Assessment and recommendation report; no obligation to change.
  • Ongoing advisory — Periodic review and support for contract renewals and new services.
  • Procurement support — Assistance with tender or quote comparison and transition.

Typical engagements are scoped after an initial consultation. Contact us to discuss.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you sell telco services directly?

We advise and compare; we do not sell carrier services ourselves. We work with multiple providers so you get options, not a single product push.

What if we're in contract?

We can still review your arrangements and plan for contract end or discuss options (e.g. early exit) so you're ready when the time comes.

Which areas do you cover?

We work with businesses across Australia—Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra, Hobart and major suburbs. Remote and regional can be discussed.
