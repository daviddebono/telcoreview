Privacy Policy

This policy describes how we collect and use information when you use our website and submit an enquiry or sign up to our newsletter.

Information we collect

When you submit the contact form, we collect your name, email address, phone number and message. If you sign up to a newsletter, we collect your email address (and any other fields we specify). We may also collect technical data such as IP address or browser type where our hosting or form provider does so for security or abuse prevention.

How we use it

We use your contact details to respond to your enquiry and to communicate with you about our services. Newsletter signups are used to send you updates and marketing communications; you can unsubscribe at any time. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Disclosure

Form submissions are forwarded to our internal team (email addresses are not exposed on the public site). We may share information with service providers who assist us (e.g. email delivery); they are required to protect your information.

Security and retention

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. We retain enquiry data for as long as needed to fulfil the purpose and for legitimate business and legal purposes.

Your rights

You may request access to or correction of your personal information, or ask us to delete it where we have no ongoing need. Contact us via the website to make a request.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version will be published on this page.
