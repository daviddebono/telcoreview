Case Studies

The following scenarios are illustrative only. They describe typical types of engagement and outcomes; they are not testimonials from specific clients. Actual results depend on your situation and location (e.g. Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra, Hobart).

Contract renewal review

A mid-size business in Melbourne was approaching the end of its phone and internet contracts. They wanted to compare alternatives without bias. We provided a capability-based comparison of several providers; they chose a new arrangement that better matched their sites and growth plans. Single-bill aggregation simplified their admin.

Contact centre and 1300 numbers

An Adelaide-based organisation needed to replace an ageing contact centre platform and rationalise 1300/1800 numbers. We compared options from multiple vendors and helped them evaluate fit, cost and transition effort. They selected a new platform and consolidated inbound numbers with clearer reporting.

Mobile fleet consolidation

A Brisbane business had mobile plans spread across several carriers. We reviewed usage and compared business plans; they consolidated to one provider with a single bill and clearer visibility of usage. Outcomes included simpler procurement and easier policy compliance.
